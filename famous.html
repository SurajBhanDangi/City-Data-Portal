Bhopal Religion 2011

In Bhopal city, Christinity is followed by 1.12 %, Jainism by 1.35 %, Sikhism by 0.57 % and Buddhism by 0.57 %. Around 0.02 % stated 'Other Religion', approximately 0.37 % stated 'No Particular Religion'.
